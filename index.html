Helen Zhou

hlzhou [at] andrew.cmu.edu

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Machine Learning Department at Carnegie Mellon University. I pursue research in machine learning, natural language processing, and their applications to healthcare.

Prior to CMU, I completed my masters of engineering (M.Eng.) degree at MIT, advised by David Sontag in the Clinical Machine Learning Group.

In my undergraduate years, I researched at the MIT Media Lab with Soroush Vosoughi and Deb Roy in the Laboratory for Social Machines, and collaborated with Scott Greenwald and Pattie Maes in the Fluid Interfaces Group.
